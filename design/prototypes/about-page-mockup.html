About Deborah Cruz

The truth-teller behind the mom goggles.

Deborah Cruz is a first-generation Mexican American writer, mother, storyteller, traveler, culture watcher, and the voice behind The TRUTH About Motherhood.

The Story

From baby years to launching-pad motherhood.

Writer first. Mother always.

Deborah built The TRUTH About Motherhood as a place to say the honest thing out loud: the funny, complicated, beautiful, messy parts of raising children and becoming yourself again in the process.

Rooted in culture.

A freckled Mexican from Chicago and first-generation Mexican American, Deborah writes through the lens of bicultural identity, family, language, food, travel, and the lived experience of raising daughters.

Still evolving.

The site began in the early motherhood years. Today, it speaks to Launching Pad Moms, Gen X women, ADHD moms, perimenopause, wellness, travel, politics, identity, and the sharp honest takes readers come back for.

“I tell the truth, all of it — the good, the bad, the ugly, and the funny.”
